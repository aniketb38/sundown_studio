Puma SE is a German multinational corporation who design and manufacture athletic and casual footwear, apparel, and accessories.

ASICS Corporation is a Japanese multinational corporation that produces sportswear. The name is an acronym for the Latin phrase anima sana in corpore sano. Asics is best known for its sneakers, but also produces other footwear such as sandals, as well as clothing.
